A _fork_ is a [diamond](/classes/diamond) that is [diagonally directed](/glossary/diagonally-directed). The name comes from the fact that these polyominoes (for small sizes) look like a forked snake tongue or a fork in the road.

A fork without punctures is also called [directed convex].
